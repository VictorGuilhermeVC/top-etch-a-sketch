My Etch A Sketch

How does it work?

  1. Hover your mouse over the Etch a Sketch screen to draw
  2. Click on "Rainbow Mode" to draw in randomly generated colors
  3. Click on "Drawing Mode" to return to default drawing mode
  4. Click on "Grid Size" to select how many squares the Etch a Sketch screen will have
  5. Click "Reset" to clear up the Drawing
  6. Have Fun! :)
